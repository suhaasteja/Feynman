Feynman.

An interactive audiobook you can interrupt with your voice to ask questions — and get answers grounded in what you're reading.

Learn anything the Feynman way: don't sit through it — stop and ask.

The problem

Listening is passive.

You hit a sentence you don't understand — a term, a claim, a leap in logic — and there's nothing you can do but rewind, or open six tabs and lose the thread entirely.

Real understanding comes from asking questions the moment they appear.

The idea

"If you can't interrupt and ask, you're not learning — you're just being read to."

Feynman narrates any article to you. Hold a key, ask out loud, hear a grounded answer in the flow — then narration picks up right where it left off.

The experience

Paste → listen → interrupt → learn

Paste an article→ It narrates→ Hold Q, ask aloud→ Grounded answer→ Resumes
  • Push-to-talk barge-in — the mic is live only while you hold the key
  • Full-duplex voice Q&A, then a natural "back to the article" lead-in
Under the hood

The stack

Convex

Reactive state, file storage, and a position-aware audio prefetch queue — no server to babysit.

OpenAI Realtime

Full-duplex spoken Q&A over WebRTC with ephemeral tokens; push-to-talk turns.

ElevenLabs

Narration TTS, generated a paragraph at a time for natural prosody.

Respan

LLM gateway for text-cleaning + the groundedness evals — with a dashboard.

Trust, not vibes

Grounded — and measured.

  • Answers are restricted to the article's own paragraphs. No outside facts, no hallucinated tangents.
  • Every answer is scored by a Respan LLM-as-judge for groundedness — asynchronously, off the critical path.
  • Scores land on each turn and in the Respan dashboard — so "is it faithful?" is a number, not a hope.
Why it works

The loop is the point

Interruption is a first-class action, not an afterthought. Barge-in cancels cleanly, grounding is re-injected on every question, and narration resumes at the paragraph you left — so curiosity never costs you your place.

Ephemeral while live, grounded on every turn, gapless on return.
